Privacy Policy

Prayer Times does not collect, share, or sell any user infomation. Prayer Times uses Apple services (Apple Privacy Policy) in order to show the name (e.g. "Cupertino, CA") of a selected location. Prayer Times does not interact with any remote servers other than Apple's.
Users who have opted into Share Analytics with App Developers in the Privacy tab in Settings should refer to the Apple Privacy Policy. Analytics provided to Prayer Times developers from this mechanism are not shared with outside parties.
